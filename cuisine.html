A Taste of the West

Alberta Beef

World-renowned for its quality and flavour, Alberta beef is the cornerstone of the province's culinary identity. A visit isn't complete without trying a local steak.

Bison

A leaner, richer alternative to beef, bison has deep roots in the prairies. It's often found in burgers, steaks, and stews, offering a true taste of the historic plains.

Saskatoon Berries

These sweet, nutty berries are a quintessential prairie treat. Look for them in pies, tarts, jams, and even drizzled over ice cream.

Craft Beer & Spirits

Alberta's craft scene is booming. Local breweries and distilleries use prairie grains and glacial water to create unique, award-winning beverages.
