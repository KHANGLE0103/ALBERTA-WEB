Frontier & Ranching Spirit

The arrival of ranchers and farmers in the late 19th century created a unique "cowboy culture" that celebrates independence, resilience, and a deep connection to the land. This spirit is famously celebrated at the Calgary Stampede.

Modern Multiculturalism

Today, Alberta is a dynamic and multicultural mosaic. People from all over the world have made Alberta their home, contributing to a thriving mix of languages, traditions, and cuisines in its vibrant cities and communities.
