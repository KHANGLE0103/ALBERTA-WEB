A Land of Breathtaking Diversity

The Rocky Mountains

Home to Banff and Jasper National Parks, the Rockies offer stunning vistas, glacier-fed lakes, and year-round outdoor adventures.

The Badlands

Step back in time in the Drumheller region, a world-renowned dinosaur hotspot with surreal canyons, coulees, and hoodoos.

Prairies & Parkland

The agricultural heartland of Alberta, this region is known for its "big sky" country, vast fields of wheat and canola, and its cattle industry.
