Everything to Know About Alberta

Capital: Edmonton
Largest City: Calgary
Population: ~4.4 Million
Motto: Fortis et Liber (Strong and Free)

Alberta is a province in Western Canada, celebrated for its dramatic landscapes, rich natural resources, and dynamic cities. It's a land of contrasts, where soaring mountain peaks give way to endless golden plains.
