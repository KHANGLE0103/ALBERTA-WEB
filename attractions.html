Must-See Attractions

Banff & Jasper National Parks

Drive the spectacular Icefields Parkway and spot wildlife like bears, elk, and bighorn sheep in these world-famous parks.

The Calgary Stampede

Experience "The Greatest Outdoor Show on Earth" with rodeos, concerts, and midway fun every July.

Royal Tyrrell Museum

Wander among incredible dinosaur skeletons and learn about Alberta's prehistoric past in the heart of the Badlands.

Northern Lights

Head to northern Alberta's dark sky preserves, like Wood Buffalo National Park, for a chance to witness the magical Aurora Borealis.
